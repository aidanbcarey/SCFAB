Checklist for Safety training: Shopbot Desktop - circuits only.

Safety Training

  1. Review of Machine Shop Safety Policy and the shop specific operating rules.
    • General Lab Safety

  2. Dangers associated with specific tools and machinery
    • Hands can be injured if job runs while hand is near the cutting bed:
      • Always run with shield.
      • One person should be in charge so that one does not start the machine without the other's awareness.
    • Injury can occur when bit breaks. Always run with shield in place.

  3. Location, requirements, and wearing of PPE
    • Eye protection must be worn. Goggles are near the lab entry.

  4. Safe use of equipment including hazards and limitations of equipment
    • Hazards
      • Flying debris or broken tools - use shield.
      • Injury to hand by spinning tool - don't run with hand near tool - use shield.
    • Limitations
      • Speed.
      • Depth of cut.
      • For circuitboard cutting, limited to 1/8" collet and tools.

  5. Demonstrate guard placement and adjustments, relevant cleaning and maintence
    • Spindle key attached to wrench to forstall some accidents.
    • Shield placement.
    • Vacuuming of dust.
    • Keep surrounding area clean and organized.

  6. Demonstrate safe equipment use
    • Demonstrate changing tool
      • Demonstrate the operation of the collet.
      • Show how to zero the bit on the work surface.
      • Show how to use wrench to tighten collet correctly.
    • Show how to run a job. Put shield in place.
    • How to stop a job.
      • Urgently:Hit the red switch
      • In a more controlled way
        • Hit the spacebar
        • When the dialog box appears, click "quit" or "resume" or adjust as appropriate.

  7. Trainee repeats main elements, demonstrates safe use of equipment.

  8. Link to EHS Machine shop safety website.
