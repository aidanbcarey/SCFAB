<br> ##d21. The ATSAMD21, an M0 32 bit device, is the microcontroller for Arduino Zero, the Adafruit metro M0, and others. - [tutorial on D21 and openocd.](https://www.omzlo.com/articles/programming-samd21-using-atmel-ice-with-openocd) - [library for ADC functions for ATSAMD21](https://github.com/Molorius/ATSAMD21-ADC). Use this to get examples of how to use the datasheet to control registers for the ADC.
