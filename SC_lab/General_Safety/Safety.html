General Lab Safety

  • Use the buddy system. Never work alone.
  • Do not get pulled into rotating machinery:
    • Tie back long hair
    • Do not wear loose clothing.
    • Do not wear jewelry.
  • Use appropriate Personal Protection (goggles, ear protection, gloves)
  • Food and drink at white tables only, not in the lab.
  • Be aware of fire exit procedures.
  • Emergency:
    • Medical/Fire: 911
    • Building: 617-495-5560
    • Public Safety: 617-495-1212
