<br> ## June 29, 2020 Using mostly summer 2020 kit materials, I want to make a moveable stage for sewing that can develop into an embroidery attachment for any machine. For this I need to sense when the needle is up, and move the stage before the needle returns. If stitching at ten stiches per second, this gives about 50 ms to move a few mm. Sounds pretty easy with stepper motor and belt drive - or maybe just a string - capstan drive. First, place magnet on steel wheel of machine and bring [magnetic sensor](https://roberthart56.github.io/SCFAB/SC_lab/Sensors/magnetic/index.html) close. Below is the setup.
